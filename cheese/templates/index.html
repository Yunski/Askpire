Our Services

Video Conferencing

In either a 1-on-1 or group setting, our clients will have the opportunity to ask questions during an hour-long consultation session with an Ivy League student mentor, asking any questions about high school planning, college application, or any other concerns they may have. The typical session involves discussions on standardized testing, high school course selection advice, academic competitions and summer programs, choosing a college major, application essay topics, and interview preparation.
